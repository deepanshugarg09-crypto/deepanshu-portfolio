Installer Events Telemetry

What this solution does

While upgrading our client from one version to another version, fresh install, architecture upgrade/downgrade, we currently raise telemetry events pre and post of installer click. Now what happens during installer is running, it was still a black box. This change in installer raises events, giving us the picture what happens during installation. At any point of time, an installer will generate either success Event or fail event with context of installer being launched.
